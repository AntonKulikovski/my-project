Giving back

Woodside Hotels is committed to operating in a responsible manner to assist the sustainability of the very special environments in which we are fortunate to live and work. We work in some very important and sensitive areas, such as along the remarkable Monterey Bay Marine Sanctuary and the beautiful Sonoma Coast, and we support a number of initiatives that help to preserve their special environments for the enjoyment of our guests, local community and associates for generations to come. Each of our hotels is proud to be an active citizen in its community and conscientious stewards of its environment.

Starting well before being ecofriendly was popular, we began programs within our hotels, restaurants and spas to improve conservation and efficiency, and have been recognized as a leading business in this effort. We have a comprehensive commitment to minimizing our impact on the environment, and our green philosophy is a core value of our company.

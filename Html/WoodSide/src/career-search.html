Search For a Job

We seek associates who are passionate about serving others and doing their jobs the best they possibly can. We’re most interested in whether candidates share our core values and have a genuine heartfelt sense of care and optimistic warmth. We believe it is easier to teach job skills, than try to instill a dedication to service and care.

We are committed to seeking and retaining associates who have a diverse set of backgrounds and experiences, but who are united by their ability to connect with others. It is vital to our day-to-day business, growth and culture that our associates respect each other and provide the type of personalized care on which our organization was founded.

Learn About Our Benefits

We offer market leading wages and competitive benefit programs to ensure that our associates are well taken care and feel supported by us. Our benefit programs include:

  • Comprehensive medical benefits with a broad-based provider network so that you can choose the plan that works best for you. Plans include medical, dental and vision insurance options
  • 401(k) retirement savings plans with employer matching so that you can plan for your retirement.
  • Paid vacation time eligibility after satisfying an introductory period.
  • Complimentary stays and discounts at other Woodside Hotels affiliated properties, restaurants and spas.

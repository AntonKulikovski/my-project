ABOUT WOODSIDE

For over four decades, Woodside Hotels has been Northern California’s premier, independent hospitality company. Founded on the simple idea of genuine, thoughtful hospitality inspired by the local land and lifestyle, we operate a collection of distinct hotels, restaurants and spas across this beautiful region.

Unlike the big hotel brands, we welcome you to gracious and unique properties with a warmth that makes you feel like you’re returning home. We delight you with handcrafted treats from our kitchens and nurturing spa treatments with ingredients cultivated from our gardens. We create insider experiences that allow you to connect with the region and bond with your family and friends. Whether you’re traveling for business or pleasure – retreat into the moment with Woodside Hotels.

Today, Woodside Hotels is stewarded by the second generation of family leaders, who grew up among these magical getaways across Northern California – running free along the Sonoma coastline, Lafayette oaks and Yountville vineyards. Likewise, the families of our first guests are returning with children of their own to share the same local experiences – and the same sense of welcome and wonder.

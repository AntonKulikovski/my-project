Best Rate Guarantee

We want you to book with confidence, and therefore guarantee that you will obtain the best rates for our hotels by booking directly on our website.

If you find a lower published rate within 24 hours of making your reservation on this website, for the same date(s) and room type, simply call and tell us the website address and we will match that site's rate.*

To report a rate discrepancy, please call the hotel at which you have booked directly between 7am and 7pm, Pacific Time, at the number below:

Monterey Plaza Hotel & Spa: (831) 646-1700
Stanford Park Hotel: (650) 322-1234
Lafayette Park Hotel & Spa: (925) 283-3700
Bodega Bay Lodge: (707) 875-3525
Napa Valley Lodge: (707) 944-2468
Hotel Drisco: (415) 346-2880
Bernardus Lodge & Spa: (888) 648-9463

*This guarantee does not apply to group or negotiated rates, or to rates found on opaque websites such as Hotwire.com. If changes are made to the reservation after booking, the rate is subject to change to the prevailing rate at that time. Further restrictions may apply.
